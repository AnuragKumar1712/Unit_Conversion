Unit Conversions
Temperature




To convert celsuis to fahrenheit yourself, use this formula replacing the `C` with your temperature in celsuis: (C x 9/5) + 32
Weight




To convert kilograms to pounds yourself, use this formula replacing the `kg` with your weight in kilograms: kg x 2.2046
Distance




To convert kilometers to miles yourself, use this formula replacing the `km` with your distance in kilometers: km ÷ 1.60934
